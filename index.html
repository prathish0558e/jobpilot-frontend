🔒 Privacy Policy
Effective January 1, 2025
JobPilot Privacy Policy

1. Data We Collect
Name, email, resume, job preferences, and automation settings. Gmail is accessed read-only for job reply emails only — we never read, store, or send personal emails.

2. How We Use Data
Solely to power JobPilot — finding jobs, sending application emails, building resumes, and showing relevant replies. We never sell or share your data.

3. Security
All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256). Resumes deleted on account deletion. Compliant with GDPR, CCPA, and Indian IT Act 2000.

4. Third Parties
Google OAuth (read-only scope), Adzuna (public job data), Google Places (location). No personal data shared.

5. Your Rights
Export, modify, or delete all data anytime from Settings. Contact: [email protected]
